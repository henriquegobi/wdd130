Our Rafting Trips

At Splash Rafting, we offer a variety of trips in South Brazil to suit every adventurer. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced rafter, we have the perfect trip for you!

Popular Trips

Apuama

The Apuama Rafting experience on the Rio Cubatão in Santo Amaro da Imperatriz, near Florianópolis, offers an exhilarating adventure for both beginners and seasoned rafters. This 3 km stretch of river features a mix of calm waters and challenging rapids, providing an ideal setting for those seeking both excitement and natural beauty.

Mambucaba

The Mambucaba River, nestled within the Serra da Bocaina National Park between Paraty and Angra dos Reis, offers an exhilarating white-water rafting experience amidst Brazil's lush Atlantic Forest. Originating at an altitude of 2,000 meters in São Paulo, the river descends through a series of rapids and waterfalls before emptying into the Atlantic Ocean, creating ideal conditions for adventure seekers.
